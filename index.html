The official 100% checklist. Five categories: 69 story missions, 20 specific Strangers & Freaks, 42 of 61 Hobbies & Pastimes, 14 of ~60 Random Events, and 16 of 30 Misc tasks. Completing it unlocks the Career Criminal trophy. Pick ending C ("Something Sensible") — endings A/B lock you out of post-story content needed for 100%. Keep a manual save after "The Big Score." Gold medals are NOT required for 100% (they're for Solid Gold, Baby!).
All 78 PS5 trophies. Story + Online combined into one bar. Online trophies are tagged ONLINE. The platinum (Los Santos Legend) requires the Online trophies too — including Rank 100, Numero Uno, Criminal Mastermind, and Decorated, which together are the bulk of the ~150+ hr platinum. A few story trophies are missable; flagged in red.
GTA Online has no "100%." It's open-ended — ranks, unlocks, and activities with no defined endpoint, and much of it requires other players or a paid grind. This tab tracks the finite Online trophies only (the same ones counted in the All Trophies bar), grouped by type so you can see what's left for the platinum. It does not feed the orange 100% bar, since Online isn't part of story completion. Progress here is also reflected in the gold Trophy bar.
